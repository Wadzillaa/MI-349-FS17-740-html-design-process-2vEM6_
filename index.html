Space Pioneers

Allowing every man to go where no man has gone before.

At Space Pioneers, our mission is to enable every person with the chance to see the stars, up close and personal. Using current rocket and aviation technology, we seek to create a business model in which we are able to send a craft with passengers into orbit around Earth for a couple of hours for a fee of around $1,000 dollars per person.

Personas

  • Children, to inspire love for math and science.
  • Adults, 25-40 interested in seeing the stars.
  • Scientists and researchers, perhaps hoping to use time in orbit to gather some type of data.

"Comp" Ideas

  • Nasa orbiting stations
  • SpaceX, relandable rockets
  • Blue Origin space tourism business model
